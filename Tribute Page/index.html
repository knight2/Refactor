Bhumibol Adulyadej

King Bhumibol the Great - Rama IX

Key events in the life of Bhumibol Adulyadej

  • 1927 - Born on 5 December 1927 at Cambridge Hospital in Cambridge, Massachusetts USA, the only monarch born in the US as his father, Prince Mahidol Adulyadej, was enrolled in the public health program at Harvard University.
  • 1946 - He ascended the throne after his brother, King Ananda Mahidol, was found dead in his palace bedchamber from a bullet wound to the head on 9 June 1946.
  • Hobbies included photography, painting, writing, music, and firearms.
"Education can be divided into two kinds of knowledge: first is the academic knowledge, which will be beneficial to oneself and the country, if applied after completing the course of learning; second is moral knowledge, or 'Dhamma', that is, learning how to conduct oneself. Both require wisdom on the part of the learner. But those who use only the academic knowledge, without moral knowledge, cannot be considered persons of wisdom."
Bhumibol Adulyadej
